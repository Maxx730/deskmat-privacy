Overview

DeskMat is a macOS application developed by John Kinghorn. This policy explains what data the app uses and how it is handled. The short version: your data stays on your Mac and is never sold or shared.

🌤️

Location — Weather Widget

If you use the weather widget, a latitude and longitude coordinate is stored locally on your device to fetch weather data. This coordinate is saved in the app's preferences and is never transmitted to any server controlled by the developer.

Weather data is fetched from a third-party weather API using your stored coordinates. Please refer to that service's privacy policy for details on how they handle request data.

📈

Stock Ticker Symbols

If you use the stock widget, the ticker symbols you enter are stored locally on your device and used to fetch market data from a third-party financial data service. These symbols are not transmitted to any server controlled by the developer.

🗂️

App Icons & Shortcuts

Icons and shortcuts you add to your dock are stored locally on your device only. This data never leaves your Mac.

🛡️

Data Not Collected

No account or sign-in required No analytics or crash reporting No advertising identifiers No data sold to third parties No personal information shared
💳

Purchases

DeskMat Pro is a one-time in-app purchase processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. The developer does not have access to your payment information. Purchase records are managed by Apple and tied to your Apple ID.

See Apple's Privacy Policy for details.

💾

Data Storage

All app data — preferences, shortcuts, cached icons, and location coordinates — is stored locally on your device using macOS standard storage mechanisms. No data is stored on external servers controlled by the developer.

👶

Children's Privacy

DeskMat does not knowingly collect any data from children under 13.

🔄

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes materially, the updated policy will be posted at this URL with a revised date.
